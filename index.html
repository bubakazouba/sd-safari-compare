Real Saturday slots scraped from tickets.sandiegozoo.org · Saturday is Peak pricing tier (typically +$26 over the marketing price). Admission ~$76 adult separately.

🦒 timing strategy (your 11am–5pm window)

  • animals most active 8–11am + after 4pm; midday they nap
  • your 11am arrival misses the morning peak entirely → save the truck for the 3:30–4:30pm slot
  • midday: shaded indoor exhibits (Hidden Jungle aviary, Gorilla Forest)
  • last Africa Tram leaves ~4:15pm

🦽 mobility (PRP)

  • rent ECV scooter at entrance — limited inventory, get it AT 11am arrival
  • ADA shuttle every 15–20min between Guest Services + Okavango Outpost
  • Easy Access Pass for Africa Tram (skips queue) — ask at tram host podium
  • free admission for ONE personal attendant
  • call 619-718-3000 in advance to book accessible truck/cart

⚠ skip-warnings (per reddit / forums)

  • Cart Safari ≈ 90% same content as the free Africa Tram — locals say don't pay
  • Balloon Safari = waste, doesn't get closer to animals
  • park food is bad — except The Watering Hole at Kijamii Overlook (rhino + giraffe view, book ahead)
  • Africa Tram lines hit 75min by 11:30am — Easy Access Pass essential

🎁 free experiences worth doing

  • Africa Tram — sit LEFT for giraffes, RIGHT for rhinos (25min)
  • Elephant Valley — NEW Mar 2026, walking bridge through habitat
  • Hidden Jungle aviary — climate-controlled midday refuge
  • Cheetah Run — check daily schedule on Safari Park app
